Audio Quality Evaluation

In the question set 1 to 12, a list of recordings are organized in rows. In each row, you will see an audio clip. Please listen to the recording and rate its audio quality using the scale: "Excellent", "Good", 'Fair", "Poor", and "Bad". For example, a "Good" sound clip would be one with some amount of distortion, but the distortion is not annoying.

To give you a better idea about how to rate, reference samples for each of these categories are given in the below table. It is essential that you refer to them before making your choice.

Audio quality Level of distortion Reference Clip A Reference Clip B
Excellent Imperceptible
Good Just perceptible, but not annoying
Fair Perceptible and slightly annoying
Poor Annoying, but not objectionable
Bad Very annoying and objectionable
  • You may listen to each clip more than once if needed; just click the play button to replay.
  • Your responses will be manually screened to ensure that you made a serious effort to rate the recordings. If your answers are determined to be random or contain inconsistent answers, the task may be rejected and you may not receive payment
  • Please perform these tasks in a quiet place using a pair of good headphones. It is recommended that you set the volume a bit higher than normal for these tasks.
  • If you are having trouble viewing or listening to the sound clips, please try completing the test in Google Chrome.
  • Please make sure that you've finished all the questions before submitting.
